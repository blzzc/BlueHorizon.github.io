Marine disaster

Marine disasters are catastrophic events that occur in maritime environments, encompassing a wide range of incidents from shipwrecks and collisions to environmental disasters like oil spills. These events have profound impacts on ecosystems, economies, and human lives. Historically, they have led to significant loss of life and environmental damage.
